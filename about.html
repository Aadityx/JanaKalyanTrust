Our Story

Who We Are

Jan Kalyan Trust (JKT) is a grassroots non-governmental organization based in Mahad Taluka, Raigad District, Maharashtra. We are dedicated to the holistic development of Kathkari tribal communities and other socio-economically disadvantaged groups.

Over nearly two decades, we have worked tirelessly to bridge the gap between marginalized communities and mainstream development — ensuring every individual has access to education, sustainable livelihoods, and a dignified life.

Our Mission

To empower Kathkari tribal communities through sustainable education, livelihood programs, and community-driven development, fostering self-reliance and dignity.

Our Vision

A society where every tribal community thrives with equal access to opportunities, education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihoods — free from marginalization.

Our Values

Compassion, transparency, community ownership, sustainability, and inclusivity guide every initiative we undertake for tribal welfare.

Our Journey

Milestones Along the Way

1998 — The Humble Beginning

In a region where educational opportunities were scarce and tribal families migrated seasonally for survival, Jankalyan Trust established its Boarding Home with just 5 students — providing a stable environment for children to receive care, education, and hope for a better future.

Early 2000s — Building Trust, One Family at a Time

The Trust actively reached out to parents and children in remote tribal settlements, encouraging them to embrace education and join the boarding program. Word spread through communities as the positive impact of the initiative became undeniable.

Growing Years — A Second Generation Arrives

A remarkable milestone was reached — children of former boarders began enrolling at the Boarding Home. This generational trust reflected the deep confidence families had placed in the institution, validating decades of dedicated service.

Peak Years — 170 Students Strong

At its peak, the Boarding Home accommodated nearly 170 students in a single year, becoming a vital center for learning, holistic development, and personal growth. Over 1,000 students have since completed their journey with us.

2024 — Silver Jubilee Celebration

Jankalyan Trust proudly celebrated the Silver Jubilee of the Boarding Home, marking 25 years of dedicated service. More than 300 former students, supporters, and well-wishers gathered for a memorable reunion celebrating the institution's enduring impact on countless lives.

Alumni Impact — Serving Across the Globe

Today, our alumni serve society in diverse fields — MIDC industries, social work, banking, teaching, entrepreneurship, and skilled trades. Some have secured opportunities abroad, including in the USA and Dubai. Their achievements are a testament to the transformative power of education.

Today — Nurturing 50 Dreams

Currently, the Boarding Home provides a safe, nurturing, and educational environment for 50 children from tribal and weaker socio-economic backgrounds. Beyond academics, we focus on holistic development, character formation, life skills, and values — empowering children to become confident, responsible, and self-reliant individuals.
