Residential Education

Building Tomorrow's Leaders

Jan Kalyan Boarding is a residential educational facility for Kathkari tribal children who lack access to quality education in their remote villages. Here, children receive not just academic instruction but holistic development — including sports, arts, agricultural exposure, and value education.

Our boarding ensures that every child has access to nutritious meals, a safe living environment, mentorship, and the opportunity to dream beyond the limitations of their circumstances.

What We Offer

Holistic Development Programs

Every aspect of a child's growth is nurtured at Jan Kalyan Boarding.

Edu@eL Classes

Technology-enhanced learning sessions using digital tools, educational software, and e-learning platforms to make education engaging and effective.

Regular Classes

Structured academic curriculum following state board guidelines with personalized attention and supplementary support for every student.

Sports & Games

Daily sports activities including kabaddi, athletics, kho-kho, football, and cricket — building teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness.

Agricultural Exposure

Hands-on farming experience at our agro farm, teaching children sustainable agriculture and connecting them with their rural roots.

Physical Fitness

Morning yoga sessions, fitness routines, and health check-ups ensuring children maintain optimal physical well-being throughout the year.

Arts & Culture

Creative workshops in drawing, music, traditional dance, and craft-making — preserving tribal culture while encouraging artistic expression.

A Day at the Boarding

Life at Jan Kalyan

6:00 AM — Rising Time

Children wake up and begin their morning routine.

6:30 AM — Morning General Exercise / Yoga

Children participate in morning exercises and yoga to start the day with energy and mindfulness

7:00 AM — Breakfast

Nutritious breakfast followed by self-study time where children revise lessons and complete homework.

7:30 AM — General Cleaning

Children clean their rooms and personal items, maintaining a hygienic environment.

8:00 AM — Study Time

Children engage in self-study sessions to revise lessons and complete homework.

9:00 AM — Schooling

Regular curriculum classes covering Mathematics, Science, Languages, Social Studies, and more.

1:00 PM — Lunch

Wholesome lunch with balanced nutrition, followed by a short rest period for the children.

4:30 PM — Evening Tea/Snacks

Children enjoy a healthy snack and tea in the evening.

5:00 PM — Sports & Games

Outdoor sports, team games, and physical fitness activities for holistic health development.

6:30 PM — Study Time

Children have a second study session to complete homework, prepare for exams, and receive tutoring support.

8:00 PM — Dinner

Wholesome dinner followed by a brief period of relaxation.

9:00 PM — Bed Time

Children get ready for the night and go to bed, ensuring a good night's sleep.
