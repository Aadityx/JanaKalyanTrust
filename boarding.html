Residential Education

Building Tomorrow's Leaders

Jan Kalyan Boarding is a residential facility for boys from the Katkari tribe and other weaker sections of the society, giving priority to the 1st generation learners who lack quality education. Here, children receive holistic support, including Edu@eL classes, life skill training, and sports.

Our boarding ensures that every child has access to nutritious meals, a safe living environment, mentorship, and the opportunity to dream beyond the limitations of their circumstances.

What We Offer

Holistic Development Programs

Every aspect of a child's growth is nurtured at Jan Kalyan Boarding.

Edu@eL Classes

Technology-enhanced learning sessions using digital tools, educational software, and e-learning platforms to make education engaging and effective.

Regular Classes

Structured academic curriculum following state board guidelines with personalized attention and supplementary support for every student.

Sports & Games

Daily sports activities including kabaddi, athletics, kho-kho, football, and cricket — building teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness.

Agricultural Exposure

Hands-on farming experience at our agro farm, teaching children sustainable agriculture and connecting them with their rural roots.

Physical Fitness

Morning yoga sessions, fitness routines, and health check-ups ensuring children maintain optimal physical well-being throughout the year.

Arts & Culture

Creative workshops in drawing, music, traditional dance, and craft-making — preserving tribal culture while encouraging artistic expression.

A Day at the Boarding

Life at Jan Kalyan

6:00 AM — Rising (Freshening)

Students wake up and freshen up to begin their morning routine.

6:20 AM — Exercise & Yoga

Morning exercises and yoga sessions to promote fitness and mindfulness.

7:00 AM — General Cleaning

Students clean their rooms and common areas, learning collective responsibility.

7:20 AM — Bath

Time allocated for personal hygiene and bathing.

7:50 AM — Breakfast

A wholesome and nourishing breakfast to fuel the boys for school preparation.

8:20 AM — Study Time

Dedicated morning self-study session to prepare for school lessons.

9:00 AM — Leave for School

Students in grades Std. 8 to 10 depart for their nearby school classes.

5:00 PM — Tea, Gardening & Sports

Students return from school for hot tea, followed by organic gardening chores and sports.

6:00 PM — Bath

Freshening up after outdoor activities and gardening.

6:30 PM — Studies

Structured evening study time for revising subjects, doing homework, and tutorials.

8:30 PM — Dinner

A freshly cooked and balanced evening meal for all boarding students.

9:15 PM — Recreation & Personal Study

Indoor games, group discussion, reading, or relaxation before bedtime.

10:00 PM — Sleep

Lights out to ensure a healthy and restorative sleep cycle.
