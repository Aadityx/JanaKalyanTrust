Programs & Initiatives

Comprehensive programs designed to create lasting impact across education, environment, and livelihood.

Home / Programs & Initiatives
Education

Nurturing Young Minds

Our education initiatives are designed to bridge the learning gap for tribal children, providing them with supplementary academic support, life skills, and a platform to voice their ideas.

Supplementary Classes

After-school educational support for children across multiple villages, covering core subjects and building foundational learning skills.

Children's Parliament

A unique platform where children learn democratic values, public speaking, and community leadership through mock governance structures.

Adolescent Groups

Focused sessions for teenagers covering life skills, health awareness, career guidance, and personal development.

Educational & Experiential Learning

Blending school curriculum with practical, hands-on learning experiences, creative arts, and basic digital literacy to foster curiosity and holistic development.

Environment

Protecting Our Planet

We believe in creating environmental consciousness among tribal communities, fostering practices that sustain both people and the planet.

Cleanliness Drives

Regular village-wide cleanliness campaigns promoting hygiene, waste management, and community pride in clean living spaces.

Nature Awareness Programs

Interactive sessions and field visits educating communities about biodiversity, conservation, and sustainable resource use.

Eco-Friendly Practices

Training communities in organic farming, rainwater harvesting, and renewable energy adoption for sustainable living.

Women Empowerment

Self Help Groups

Our SHG program is a cornerstone of women's empowerment, enabling tribal women to achieve financial independence, community leadership, and self-confidence.

Savings Culture

Weekly savings groups enabling women to build financial security, access micro-credit, and fund personal and family needs.

Financial Literacy

Workshops on budgeting, banking, investment, and business management — empowering women with economic knowledge.

Micro-Enterprises

Supporting women to start small businesses including tailoring, food processing, and handcraft production for additional income.

Regular Meetings & Community Initiatives

Organising periodic meetings to foster unity, discuss local issues, and spearhead community-led initiatives such as cleanliness drives and awareness campaigns.

Sustainable Livelihoods

Livelihood Programs

Our livelihood and skill development programs open doors to sustainable incomes and dignity, specifically tailored to the opportunities and resources of tribal youth and women.

Traditional Livelihoods & Agro Products

Supporting sustainable farming and marketing of traditional tribal goods, agro-farm products (such as paddy, turmeric), and pure forest honey.

Poultry & Goat Farming (Goatry)

Providing essential livestock, cages, and training in goatry and poultry rearing to establish reliable secondary income streams for rural households.

Herbal Medicines & Forest Produce

Empowering communities to utilize local knowledge for harvesting, processing, and selling natural herbal remedies and traditional products.

Vocational Training & Skill Development

Practical courses in trades like masonry, plumbing, and tailoring that build career readiness and foster self-reliance among youth and women.
