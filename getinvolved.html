Donate

Your Donation, Their Future

Every rupee you donate directly impacts a tribal family's access to education, healthcare, nutrition, and livelihood. Jan Kalyan Trust is committed to full transparency — you'll know exactly where your contribution goes.

₹2,500/month

Sponsors one child's boarding education including meals, books, and activities.

₹5,000

Supports a Self Help Group for 3 months with training materials and meeting costs.

₹10,000

Funds agro farm supplies for one season — seeds, tools, and organic fertilizers.
