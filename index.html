About Us

Rooted in Compassion, Driven by Impact

Founded to uplift the most marginalized communities in rural Maharashtra, Jan Kalyan Trust has been a beacon of hope for Kathkari tribals, providing them access to education, healthcare, livelihood opportunities, and a dignified life.

Our holistic approach encompasses education through residential boarding, women's empowerment via Self Help Groups, sustainable agriculture, and skill development — ensuring lasting, generational change.

Our Key Initiatives

We work across six core areas to create sustainable, lasting impact in tribal communities.

Education

Supplementary classes, children's parliaments, and adolescent groups nurturing young minds for a brighter tomorrow.

Environment

Cleanliness drives, nature awareness programs, and eco-friendly practices promoting a sustainable future.

Self Help Groups

Empowering women through savings culture, financial literacy, and community-driven initiatives.

Livelihood

Masonry, plumbing, and vocational training providing dignified employment opportunities to tribal youth.

Boarding

Jan Kalyan Boarding provides residential education with sports, agricultural exposure, and holistic development.
